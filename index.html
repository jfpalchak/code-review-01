Hello, world!

Joey Palchak graduated from the University of Vermont in 2015 with a Bachelors in Computer Science, while minoring in both Mathematics and Philosophy. Unlike his colleagues, however, he did not join the workforce immediately after graduating; instead, he packed up his '08 Subaru Impreza and traveled the country, until the car eventually brokedown in Portland, OR.

He never left.

Before joining Epicodus in 2023, Joey has worked as a bartender, a Music Director for community radio, as well as a freelance Producer for local radio and podcasts.
